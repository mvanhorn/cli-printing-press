- Social media (X, LinkedIn, TikTok, Instagram) - perpetual
- Embed on `printingpress.dev` landing page hero - perpetual

- 1.x revisions of the same edit (e.g., captions burn-in) - same rights

If your chosen track licence does not include landing-page hosting, do NOT use it on the printingpress.dev hero - X-only is acceptable but reduces reach.

## SFX

The four required SFX are off-the-shelf library sounds:

- `sfx-stamp.wav` - press / stamp impact (used 2x at frames 450 and 630)
- `sfx-glitch.wav` - RGB glitch swell (used at frame 60 in Hook)
- `sfx-ink-assemble.wav` - whoosh + settle (used at frame 1140 in CTA)
- `sfx-keystroke.wav` - single keystroke (used 6x in Solution as command types out)

Source candidates:
- [Freesound.org](https://freesound.org) - many CC0 / CC-BY options; verify each licence
- [Soundsnap](https://www.soundsnap.com) - subscription, broad commercial rights
- [Splice Sounds](https://splice.com/sounds) - subscription, broad commercial rights

If you use a CC-BY track, document attribution here:

```
Title:    [track title]
Author:   [author]
Source:   [URL]
Licence:  CC BY 4.0 (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/)
```

## Loudness target

All audio is mastered to -14 LUFS integrated (X / Spotify target). Confirm with `ffmpeg-loudnorm` or Logic Pro / Audacity loudness meter before final render.
